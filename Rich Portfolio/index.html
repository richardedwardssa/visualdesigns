Design Systems built for AI Integration
Innovation
Breakthrough

Do organizations require bespoke design systems? While comprehensive design systems—built in Figma with foundational elements, component libraries, variables, and tokens—represent a significant investment, the answer is not necessarily.

Rather than pursuing traditional custom design system development, I advocate for a more efficient approach that integrates artificial intelligence, Model Context Protocol (MCP), and intelligent design workflows.

This methodology delivers measurable advantages: reduced maintenance overhead, enhanced design consistency, automated workflows for repetitive tasks, and accelerated delivery of scalable designs, proofs of concept, and production-ready code across platforms and teams.

For organizations seeking to optimize costs, timelines, and resource allocation, I'd welcome the opportunity to discuss how this integrated approach can deliver tangible value to your operation.
